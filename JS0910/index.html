About

This page is about TV Show "Hannibal".

It was started on April 4 2013.

Developed by Bryan Fuller. Showrunner NBC.

The series is based on characters and elements appearing in Thomas Harris' novels "Red Dragon" (1981) and "Hannibal" (1999)

The show is focusing on the relationship of FBI special investigator Will Graham (Hugh Dancy) and Dr. Hannibal Lecter (Mads Mikkelsen), a forensic psychiatrist destined to become Graham's most cunning enemy
